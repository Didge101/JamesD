About James D

James D, born in 2002, hails from the west side of Rockford, IL. Starting his music career in 2016 as Didge, a member of the group G888, James rebranded in 2024 to bring his unique blend of rap and R&B to the forefront. With a strong connection to his community and a passion for storytelling, James D aims to inspire and captivate audiences through his music.
